How to play

Players roll two dice each round. With three rounds per game, the player with the highest score wins.

How to score

  • If any player rolls a one, the player scores zero for that round
  • If a player rolls two different numbers, you score the equivalent of adding those numbers together
  • If a player rolls two of the same numbers, you score the equivalent of adding those numbers together, and multiplying by two
